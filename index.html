About Us

Delivering Best Health Care in Gudur

Welcome to KV Multispeciality Hospital, where we are dedicated to providing the highest quality medical care to our patients. Our team of skilled and compassionate doctors, nurses, and support staff are here to help you feel better and get back to your best self.

We offer a wide range of services, including primary care, urgent care, specialty care, and more. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with the latest technology and equipment, ensuring that you receive the best possible care. We understand that visiting the hospital can be stressful, which is why we strive to create a welcoming and comfortable environment for our patients. We are here to listen to your concerns and provide support every step of the way.

If you or a loved one is in need of medical attention, don’t hesitate to visit us at KV Hospital. We are here to help you get the care you need and get back to living your life to the fullest.

Managing Director's Message

Dr. Trivikram Recharla [MBBS, DCH, DNB]

We Offer Different Services To Improve Your Health

Pediatrics & Neonatology

Our Pediatrics & Neonatology department provides comprehensive care for newborns and children. We ensure the best medical support for the youngest patients.

Obstetrics and Gynecology

Our Obstetrics and Gynecology department offers specialized care for women’s health. We focus on prenatal care, childbirth, and gynecological issues.

General Medicine

The General Medicine department provides diagnosis and treatment for a variety of health conditions. Our experienced physicians offer comprehensive care.

Neurology

Our Neurology department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of neurological disorders. We offer advanced care for conditions affecting the nervous system.

Pediatric Cardiology

The Pediatric and Cardiology department provides heart care for children. We focus on the diagnosis and treatment of pediatric heart conditions.

Pediatric Surgeon

Our Pediatric Surgeons are skilled in performing surgeries on children. We ensure safe and effective surgical care for our young patients.

Pediatric Neurology

The Pediatric Neurology department addresses neurological conditions in children. We offer specialized care to manage and treat these disorders.

Surgical Gastroenterologist

Our Surgical Gastroenterologists provide expert surgical care for gastrointestinal issues. We focus on minimally invasive procedures for better outcomes.
