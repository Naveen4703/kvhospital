LAMINAR FLOW OPERATION THEATRE

State of art facility ,highly advanced ,first laminar flow operation theatre in gudur,capable of doing all orthopedic,spine,neurosurgery ,gyceology ,laparoscopic surgeries. Advantages with laminar flow operation theatre

The significant reduction in airborne contaminants, including bacteria and viruses. Laminar airflow creates a continuous, unidirectional flow of highly filtered air, minimizing the presence of pathogens that could potentially cause surgical site infections.

Improved Sterility: The controlled airflow in laminar flow theatres helps maintain a sterile environment by directing particles away from the surgical site and towards exhaust vents. This helps in preventing contamination of surgical wounds and reduces the risk of post-operative complications.

Regulated Temperature and Humidity: Laminar flow systems often include mechanisms to regulate temperature and humidity levels within the operating theatre.
Improved Air Quality: The high-efficiency particulate air (HEPA) filters used in laminar flow systems effectively remove dust, allergens, and other airborne particles.
